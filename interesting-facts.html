Personality

  1. Despite his hatred and obession with killing goblins, Goblin Slayer is a kind individual.
  2. Goblin Slayer is interested in learning new ideas from people of all walks of life. He is always looking for new knowledge to absorb, mostly regarding the most efficient ways of tracking, trapping, and killing goblins.
  3. As Goblin Slayer has spent more time with his companions he has become more socially adept, thinking more about others and even making remarks involving dry humor on the rare occasion.

Goblin Slayer Abilities

  • Goblin Knowledge - He has devoted himself to all matters related to goblins, ranging from their habits to their mindsets. This makes him the foremost expert when it comes to dealing with goblins.
  • Trained Combatant - Has relative skill with most weapons that he can get his hands on, using them as effectively as possible. Still prefers a short sword and buckler due to the cramped fighting conditions of goblin lairs.
  • Endurance - Posseses an incredible amount of stamina and pain tolerance, and has been capable of fighting even after breaking multiple bones. Also is well acclimated to snowy mountains and cold temperatures, due to recieving most of his training in such environments.
